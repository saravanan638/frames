ice cream

Description:

ice cream is a sweetened frozen food typically eaten as a snack or dessert. It may be made from milk or cream and is flavoured with a sweetener, either sugar or an alternative, and a spice, such as cocoa or vanilla, or with fruit such as strawberries or peaches. It can also be made by whisking a flavored cream base and liquid nitrogen together.

ingredients of ice cream at peak dairies:

*greater than 10% milk fat
*6 to 10% milk and non-fat milk solids: this component, also known as the milk solids-not-fat or serum solids, contains the proteins (caseins and whey proteins) and carbohydrates (lactose) found in milk

It generally also has:

*12 to 16% sweeteners: usually a combination of sucrose and glucose-based corn syrup sweeteners
*0.2 to 0.5% stabilizers and emulsifiers
*55 to 64% water, which comes from the milk or other ingredients.
